Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding accommodation on EKSU Lodge.

Is browsing properties free?
Yes! Browsing and searching for properties is completely free. You only need an account to contact landlords directly.
How do I contact a landlord?
Each listing has Call and WhatsApp buttons that connect you directly to the landlord or agent — no middleman, no extra fees.
Are all listings verified?
All listings are reviewed by our admin team before going live. Landlords with the "Verified" badge have had their identity confirmed.
How do I list my property?
Click "List a Room" in the header, create a landlord account, and submit your property from your dashboard. It goes live after admin approval — usually within 24 hours.
How long does approval take?
Listings are typically reviewed within 24 hours. You can track the status in your dashboard.
Can I save listings to view later?
Yes! Click the heart icon on any listing to save it. You can view all saved listings in your dashboard under "Saved Listings".
